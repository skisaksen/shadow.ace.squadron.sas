Squadron Briefing

Who We Are

Shadow Ace Squadron is a DCS World unit built around one idea: flying is better when it means something. We run structured, story-driven air operations — carrier launches into contested airspace, strike packages with real objectives, and missions where your wingman actually matters.

We take the simulation seriously and ourselves a little less so. Briefings are real, comms are disciplined, and the debrief usually ends in laughter. Whether you're a day-one pilot who just bought your first module or a virtual thousand-hour veteran, there's a seat in the package for you.

No paywalls, no drama, no gatekeeping. Show up, fly the brief, cover your lead.
